Q: What is the process to return a product?
A: To return a product with Hey Harper, follow these steps:

1. **Access the Returns Portal**: Go to Hey Harper's website and enter their returns portal. This step is crucial for Hey Harper to acknowledge your return request.

2. **Provide Necessary Information**: Enter your order number and email/zip/phone to find your order.

3. **Prepare the Item**: Ensure the product is in its original condition, including packaging, accessories, and manuals. It should be suitable for resale.

4. **Return Label**: If available in your country, Hey Harper will provide a return label. If not, you must register the return on their platform and send the item back at your own expense.

5. **Send the Return**: Ship the item to the provided return address within 14 days from the day after you received the order.

6. **Refund Processing**: If the return is accepted, you will be reimbursed within 14 days, excluding delivery costs, via the payment method used for the purchase. 

7. **Track Refund**: Allow up to 5 business days for processing after the package is received by their warehouse, with the refund appearing in your account 1-3 days later due to the international bank transfer process.
