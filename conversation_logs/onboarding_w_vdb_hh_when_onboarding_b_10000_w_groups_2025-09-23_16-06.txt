Q: Explain what is covered by the warranty that comes with your products.
A: The warranty for Hey Harper products covers the following:

1. **Jewelry Warranty:**
   - All Hey Harper jewelry comes with a 6-month breakage warranty starting from the order delivery date.
   - To determine if an item qualifies for the breakage warranty, customers must contact Hey Harper Customer Support.
   - Photos or videos of the broken item are required for warranty assessment.
   - If the warranty applies, Hey Harper will provide a store credit, discount code for the item's value, or a replacement.
   - Items broken after the 6-month period are not covered.
   - Lost items, enamel details, beads, shells, and pearls are excluded from the warranty.
   - There is a lifetime color warranty for jewelry but it doesn't cover breakages, wear-and-tear marks, scratches, loss, or accidents, nor does it activate for slight darkening.

2. **Watch Warranty:**
   - Hey Harper watches have a Limited International Warranty for 24 months covering manufacturing defects.
   - To activate this warranty, customers must contact Hey Harper Customer Support and provide photos or videos of the issue.
   - The warranty excludes loss of the watch, non-Hey Harper watches, batteries, routine maintenance, cleaning, scratches, wear and tear, lack of care, improper maintenance, tampering, abuse, loss of color, rust, and water penetration.
   - A photo or video is mandatory to activate the warranty.
   - All warranty claims involve a careful assessment, and decisions are final.
   - Hey Harper can deny requests for watch warranty activations.

For all warranties, contact Hey Harper's Customer Support Team at info@heyharper.com for assistance.
