Q: Explain what is covered by the warranty that comes with your products.
A: The warranty coverage includes a Lifetime Color Warranty and a Breakage Warranty. The Lifetime Color Warranty covers issues related to the color of the products, while the Breakage Warranty covers breakage of items within six months from the delivery date. To activate the Breakage Warranty, you must contact the Customer Support Team with photos or videos of the broken item. If verified, either store credit/discount code or a replacement will be provided. Additionally, Hey Harper watches are covered by a Limited International Warranty against manufacturing defects for twenty-four months from the date of purchase. Lost items and items broken after six months are not covered by the breakage warranty.

Q: Explain what to do if the product has not been received.
A: If the product has not been received, the following steps should be taken:

1. Check for a confirmation email to ensure the order was dispatched and note the order number provided in the order confirmation email.

3. If there's an issue with the dispatch, send an email to info@heyharper.com to inquire about updates.
4. If the order is marked as delivered by the carrier, but you haven't received it, contact Hey Harper’s Customer Service promptly within 15 days of the delivery date.
5. Make a claim within this period; after 15 days, the support team will no longer accept a claim for non-delivery.

Q: Explain what to do if a product keeps breaking.
A: If a product keeps breaking, follow these steps:

1. Check if the breakage occurred within the warranty period. This period starts from the delivery date of your order and the breakage warranty does not cover items broken after six months from receipt.

2. Contact the Customer Support Team to inquire if your item qualifies for the Breakage Warranty.

3. Provide the Customer Support Team with mandatory evidence, such as photos or videos, clearly showing the broken item.

4. If the team verifies the issue, you may receive a store credit/discount code equal to the item's value or a replacement for the item.

Note that the breakage warranty does not cover lost items.
