Q: What is the process to return a product?
A: To return a product, follow these steps:

1. Ensure eligibility: Confirm that the product is suitable for return. Items not suitable for return include those that are unsealed due to health protection or hygiene reasons, and personalized products. Items must be in their original, unused condition, unworn, with all tags/security seals attached.

2. Initiate the return: Within 14 days of receiving the order, request a return through Hey Harper's returns portal on their website.

3. Receive return instructions: Hey Harper will acknowledge your return request and provide a return label if available in your country. If not, you'll need to register the return on the platform and ship the item back at your expense.

4. Ship the product: Ensure the product is in its original condition, suitable for resale, and ship it to the provided return address.

5. Refund process: If the return is accepted, Hey Harper will reimburse you for all payments made within 14 days, excluding delivery costs. Defective or non-conforming products can be returned free of charge. 

Returns made after 14 days or items that do not meet the conditions may be refused or partially refunded. The right to return does not apply to worn or damaged items, or those missing required tags/seals.

Q: Which products, if any, require a different return process?
A: Personalized products and products not suitable for return due to health protection or hygiene reasons if unsealed after delivery require a different return process.
